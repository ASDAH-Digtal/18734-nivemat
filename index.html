What is Tiktok?

Tiktok is a video-sharing social interworking service owned by a Chinese company Byte Dance. The social media platform is used to make a variety of short, filmed videos, from genres like dance, comedy and education, that have duration of 15-60seconds. Tiktok is an international version of Douyin, which was originally released in a Chinese market in September 2016. Later, Tiktok was launched in 2017 for IOS and Android in most markets outside of the mainland china; however, it only became worldwide after merging with another Chinese social media service, Musical.ly, on 2 august 2018
